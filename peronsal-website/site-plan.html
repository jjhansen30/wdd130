Overview

Purpose

I develop websites. I have a huge drive to better myself. I want to help others better themselves as well. Have you noticed that there are a lot of people who feel as though they are just stuck in life and that they missed their opportunity to do great things, and feel like they can’t succeed at anything? That’s what I want to help fix, to build a website that will help them see that they can be successful. I want to give them the tools that will help them see themselves as amazing people.

Audience

This website is mainly for gen x'ers and millennials who are having a hard time figuring out what to do in life. They also want a convenient spot for all things self development. They also do not want to spend a lot of money on self development books. They have desires to do great things but they do not how to do it. They need organization tools and a step by step process of how to do it.
